Handwoven Rattan

Traditional & Thoughtful Production The rattan is softened with heat and shaped by skilled artisans from Central Java, Indonesia. Each product is handwoven with precision, using nails and rattan bark strips for reinforcement.
A transparent protective finish is applied to preserve its natural beauty and durability.
It takes nearly four hours to complete a single woven product, showcasing true artisan craftsmanship.

Why Choose Our Rattan Products?
✅ Sustainable & Biodegradable – A guilt-free alternative to plastic.
✅ Handcrafted & Unique – No two pieces are exactly alike.
✅ Durable & Stylish – Perfect for modern, boho, or rustic decor.
